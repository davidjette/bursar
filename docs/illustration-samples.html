Color & Editing Standards

Color Overlay

Subtle blue tint (5-10% opacity of brand accent #2563eb) to unify diverse stock images under Bursar's color palette.

Brightness

Images skew bright and modern. Avoid dark, moody, or heavily shadowed photos.

Composition

People should be visible and approachable. Avoid abstract or overly stylized compositions.

🎯 Recommendation

Use professional stock photography for all Bursar hero sections, feature images, and marketing materials.

Provider: Unsplash (free) or Shutterstock (commercial license, ~$500/yr for unlimited)
Search terms: "business team", "compliance", "organization", "data analytics", "trust"
Edit process: Slight color grade (boost brightness, add subtle blue overlay) in Figma or Photoshop
Timeline: 30 minutes to source and edit a new hero image
