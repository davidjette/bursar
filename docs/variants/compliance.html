Your Legal Obligations as a Board Member

As a California HOA board member, you're personally responsible for:

  • Annual budget disclosures Civil Code §5300
  • Reserve study updates every 3 years §5550
  • Election compliance §5100–5145
  • Meeting notice requirements §4920
  • Document requests within specific timeframes §5200
  • Insurance certificates on file and current
